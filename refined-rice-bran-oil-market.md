<p><strong>The Refined Rice Bran Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extraction</li><li>Squeezing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The refined rice bran oil market can be categorized primarily into two extraction methods: solvent extraction and mechanical squeezing. Solvent extraction involves using chemicals to efficiently extract oil from rice bran, resulting in higher yields but requiring additional refining to remove solvents. In contrast, the mechanical squeezing method, or cold pressing, extracts oil by physically pressing the rice bran, preserving more nutrients and flavor but typically yielding less oil. Both methods cater to different consumer preferences and market demands in the oil sector.</p></p>

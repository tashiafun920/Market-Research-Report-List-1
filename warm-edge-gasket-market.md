<p><p>Warm edge gaskets are specialized insulating spacers used in double or triple-glazed windows to reduce heat transfer and improve energy efficiency. By minimizing the thermal bridge created by traditional aluminum spacers, warm edge gaskets enhance a window's insulation performance, thereby contributing to lower energy costs and improved indoor comfort. These gaskets are typically made from materials like butyl rubber, silicone, or thermoplastics, and are essential for maintaining proper seal integrity and durability in insulated glazing units.</p><p>The Warm Edge Gasket Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.8% during the forecast period, driven by increasing energy conservation awareness and stringent building regulations promoting energy-efficient solutions.

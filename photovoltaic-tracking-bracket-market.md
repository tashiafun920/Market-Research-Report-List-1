<p><p>The photovoltaic tracking bracket market is primarily categorized into two types: two-row component tracking and single-row component tracking. Two-row tracking systems utilize a dual-axis movement mechanism, optimizing solar panel orientation for maximum sun exposure, thereby enhancing energy efficiency for larger installations. In contrast, single-row tracking systems are simpler, featuring a single-axis design that adjusts the panels along one direction, making them suitable for smaller installations with lower energy demands while still improving yield compared to fixed systems.</p></p>

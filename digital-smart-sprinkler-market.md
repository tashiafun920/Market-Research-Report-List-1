<p><p>The digital smart sprinkler market is categorized into fully automatic and semi-automatic systems. Fully automatic sprinklers operate autonomously, utilizing sensors and smart technology to detect soil moisture, weather conditions, and plant needs, adjusting watering schedules accordingly. In contrast, semi-automatic systems require some user intervention, where timers and programmable settings allow for scheduled watering but may not adjust dynamically to changing conditions. Both types aim to enhance water conservation and optimize irrigation efficiency for various landscaping and agricultural applications.</p></p>

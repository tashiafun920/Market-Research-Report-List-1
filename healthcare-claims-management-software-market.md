<p><p>Healthcare claims management software can be categorized into two main types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise software is installed directly on a healthcare provider's local servers, offering greater control over data security and customization but often requiring higher upfront costs and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based solutions operate remotely on the vendor's servers, providing scalability, easier updates, and lower initial investment. This type promotes accessibility and collaboration while minimizing the burden of on-site infrastructure management.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Cold Work Die Steel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Blanking die</li><li>Cold upsetting die</li><li>Cold extrusion die</li><li>Bending die</li><li>Drawing die</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The **Cold Work Die Steel Market** caters to various die types used in metalworking processes. **Blanking dies** are used to cut flat shapes from sheet metal. **Cold upsetting dies** form metal by compressing it, often for bolts or fasteners. **Cold extrusion dies** shape metal by forcing it through a die to create complex profiles. **Bending dies** are used to bend metal into specific angles or curves. **Drawing dies** pull metal through a die to form deep, hollow shapes like tubes or cups. Each die type requires durable, high-strength steel to withstand high pressures and wear.</p></p>

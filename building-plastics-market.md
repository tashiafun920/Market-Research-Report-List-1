<p><strong>The Building Plastics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermoplastics</li><li>Thermosetting Plastics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The building plastics market primarily comprises two types: thermoplastics and thermosetting plastics. Thermoplastics are materials that can be repeatedly melted and reshaped, making them ideal for applications such as pipes, insulation, and windows. They offer flexibility and durability. In contrast, thermosetting plastics undergo a chemical change when heated, forming a rigid structure that cannot be remolded. These are often used in applications requiring high strength and heat resistance, such as electrical components and adhesives, contributing to their growing market demand.</p></p>

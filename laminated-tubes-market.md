<p><strong>The Laminated Tubes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plastic Barrier Laminate (PBL)</li><li>Aluminium Barrier Laminate (ABL)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laminated tubes market consists of two primary types: Plastic Barrier Laminate (PBL) and Aluminium Barrier Laminate (ABL). PBL tubes are made from a combination of plastic layers, offering flexibility and lightweight characteristics, making them suitable for various personal care and cosmetic products. In contrast, ABL tubes incorporate aluminium layers, providing superior barrier properties against moisture and light, thereby ensuring longer shelf life for products like pharmaceuticals and food. Both types cater to diverse packaging needs across industries.</p></p>

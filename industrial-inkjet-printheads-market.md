<p><ul><li>Piezoelectric Printheads</li><li>Thermal Printheads</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial inkjet printheads market is primarily categorized into three types: piezoelectric printheads, thermal printheads, and others. Piezoelectric printheads use electric signals to generate pressure, enabling precise droplet placement, making them ideal for various viscosities and inks. Thermal printheads, on the other hand, heat the ink to create vapor bubbles, forcing droplets out with speed, commonly used for lower-viscosity fluids. The "others" category encompasses various emerging technologies and specialized printheads that cater to specific industrial applications, enhancing versatility in printing.</p></p>

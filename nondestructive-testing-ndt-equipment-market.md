<p><p>Nondestructive Testing (NDT) equipment encompasses various techniques to assess material integrity without causing damage. Laser testing utilizes focused light to detect surface imperfections, while Magnetic Flux Leakage identifies flaws in ferromagnetic materials by measuring magnetic leakage. Magnetic Particle Testing reveals surface and near-surface defects through magnetic particles applied to a material, and Ultrasonic Testing employs high-frequency sound waves to locate internal inconsistencies. These methods are essential in industries such as aerospace, manufacturing, and construction for ensuring safety and reliability.</p></p>

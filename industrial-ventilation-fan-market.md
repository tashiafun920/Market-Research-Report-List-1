<p><p>The industrial ventilation fan market comprises three primary types: wall fans, ceiling fans, and window-mounted fans. Wall fans are designed for mounting on vertical surfaces, providing targeted airflow in specific areas, ideal for large spaces. Ceiling fans circulate air efficiently across a wide area, suitable for open industrial settings. Window-mounted fans fit into window openings, facilitating the intake or exhaust of air, which is beneficial for ventilation in smaller industrial environments. Each type serves distinct purposes in optimizing air circulation and improving environmental conditions.</p></p>

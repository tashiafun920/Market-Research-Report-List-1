<p><p>The commercial masticating juicer market is primarily divided into two types: vertical auger and horizontal auger juicers. In contrast, horizontal auger commercial masticating juicers are known for their versatility and ability to handle a wider variety of produce, including leafy greens and hard vegetables. Both types emphasize nutrient preservation and yield high-quality juice in commercial settings.</p></p>

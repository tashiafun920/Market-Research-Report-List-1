<p><strong>The Automotive Grade POM Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Homopolymer POM</li><li>Copolymer POM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive grade POM (polyoxymethylene) market comprises two main types: homopolymer POM and copolymer POM. Homopolymer POM is characterized by its high strength, superior rigidity, and excellent wear resistance, making it ideal for components like gears and bearings. In contrast, copolymer POM offers enhanced impact resistance and improved toughness, suitable for applications requiring greater durability under stress. Both types are widely used in the automotive industry for parts that require low friction and high dimensional stability.</p></p>

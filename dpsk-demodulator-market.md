<p><p>The DPSK demodulator market comprises three main types: tunable, passive, and semi-tunable. Tunable demodulators allow for adjustment across various frequencies, enhancing versatility in communication systems. Passive demodulators, in contrast, operate without external power, relying on the input signal for functionality, making them simpler and more cost-effective. Semi-tunable demodulators offer a balance, allowing limited tuning while maintaining a degree of passive operation. Each type serves distinct application needs, catering to different performance and cost requirements in modern communication technologies.</p></p>

<p><p>The Transportation Composites and Plastics market is broadly categorized into thermoplastics and thermosets. Thermoplastics are materials that can be repeatedly melted and reshaped, making them ideal for large-scale production, lightweight applications, and recycling. In contrast, thermosets undergo a chemical change when cured, resulting in materials that are strong and heat-resistant but cannot be remolded. Both types serve critical roles in automotive, aerospace, and other transportation sectors, enhancing performance, fuel efficiency, and overall durability.</p></p>

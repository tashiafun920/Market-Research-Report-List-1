<p><p>The RF Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) Filters market consists of types like Transversal Filters and Resonator Filters. Transversal Filters utilize multiple interdigitated transducers (IDTs) to process signals, offering a wider frequency response, making them ideal for communication applications. In contrast, Resonator Filters rely on a single resonator structure to achieve high selectivity and lower insertion loss, suitable for niche applications requiring precise filtering. Both types play critical roles in enhancing wireless communication performance and improving signal quality.</p></p>

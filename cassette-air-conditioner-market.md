<p><p>The Cassette Air Conditioner market is categorized based on airflow configurations: Single Out Wind, Two Out Wind, and Four Out Wind. Single Out Wind units offer targeted cooling in one direction, ideal for small spaces. Two Out Wind models distribute air in two directions, providing balanced cooling for medium-sized areas. Four Out Wind systems feature vents in all directions, ensuring uniform air distribution across larger spaces for enhanced comfort. Each type caters to specific spatial requirements and user preferences.</p></p>

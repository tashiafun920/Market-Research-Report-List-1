<p><p>The heat pump dehumidifier market features mainly two types: tower type and rotor type. Tower type dehumidifiers utilize a vertical design, enhancing airflow and efficiency in moisture removal. They are ideal for larger spaces and provide consistent performance. In contrast, rotor type dehumidifiers employ a rotating desiccant wheel to absorb moisture, making them suitable for applications requiring continuous operation and lower energy consumption. Both types cater to diverse environments, offering effective humidity control in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>

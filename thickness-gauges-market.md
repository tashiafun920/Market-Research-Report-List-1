<p><ul><li>Contact Thickness Gauge</li><li>Non-Contact Thickness Gauge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thickness gauges market is segmented into two main types: contact and non-contact thickness gauges. Contact thickness gauges utilize mechanical or electronic probes to directly measure material thickness by applying pressure to the surface, making them suitable for various materials like metals and plastics. Non-contact thickness gauges, on the other hand, use technologies such as laser or ultrasound to measure thickness without physical contact, ideal for delicate or layered materials. Both types cater to different applications across industries such as manufacturing, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Electric Grease Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-priming</li><li>Submersible</li><li>Suction Head</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric grease pumps are categorized into three main types: self-priming, submersible, and suction head. Self-priming pumps can draw grease from a lower level without needing external assistance, making them versatile for various applications. Submersible pumps are designed to operate underwater, ideal for deep reservoirs or tanks. Suction head pumps rely on atmospheric pressure to facilitate grease movement, typically suited for lower viscosity products. Each type serves specific environments and operational needs within the grease distribution sector.</p></p>

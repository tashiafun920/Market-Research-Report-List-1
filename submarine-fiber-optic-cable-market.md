<p><ul><li>Single Core</li><li>Multicore</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The submarine fiber optic cable market is categorized into single core and multicore types. Single core cables consist of one optical fiber, ideal for straightforward, long-distance communication with reduced transmission loss. In contrast, multicore cables contain multiple fibers within a single cable, enabling higher data capacity and redundancy. This versatility allows multicore cables to handle substantial data traffic, making them suitable for densely populated regions and expansive networks, enhancing connectivity and bandwidth across global communications infrastructure.</p></p>

<p><p>The EMV smart card market is divided into two main types: contact and contactless forms. Contact EMV cards require physical insertion into a card reader for transactions, enhancing security through direct communication. In contrast, contactless EMV cards allow users to make payments by tapping or waving the card near a reader, offering convenience and speed. Both types are designed to comply with EMV standards, ensuring secure transactions across various sectors, including retail and transportation, while addressing consumer preferences for ease of use.</p></p>

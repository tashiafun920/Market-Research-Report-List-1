<p><p>The cosmetic natural pigment market is categorized into three main types: plant-derived pigments, mineral-extracted pigments, and animal-derived pigments. Plant-derived pigments, sourced from fruits, vegetables, and flowers, offer vibrant colors and are widely used for their natural appeal. Mineral-extracted pigments, obtained from natural minerals, provide stability and are favored for their long-lasting properties. Animal-derived pigments, sourced from sources like cochineal, deliver unique hues but are less common due to ethical considerations, focusing instead on plant and mineral alternatives.</p></p>

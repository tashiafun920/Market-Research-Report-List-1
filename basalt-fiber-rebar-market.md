<p><p>The Basalt Fiber Rebar market can be categorized into two main types: composites and non-composites. Composites incorporate basalt fibers into a resin or other matrix, enhancing strength and durability, making them suitable for high-performance applications in construction and engineering. Non-composites, on the other hand, consist solely of basalt fibers without additional binding agents, focusing on cost-effectiveness and lighter applications. Both types offer corrosion resistance and improved tensile strength, catering to diverse project needs within the construction industry.</p></p>

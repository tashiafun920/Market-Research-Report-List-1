<p><strong>The Conductive Graphene Filament Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Asphalt Based</li><li>Pan Based</li><li>Viscose Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The conductive graphene filament market comprises three main types: asphalt-based, pan-based, and viscose-based filaments. Asphalt-based filaments utilize bitumen to enhance conductivity, making them suitable for applications in construction and electronics. Pan-based filaments are derived from polyacrylonitrile and offer superior thermal stability and mechanical strength, ideal for aerospace and automotive industries. Viscose-based filaments leverage regenerated cellulose, providing a biodegradable option while maintaining decent conductivity, appealing to sustainable technology sectors. Each type addresses specific needs in various industries.</p></p>

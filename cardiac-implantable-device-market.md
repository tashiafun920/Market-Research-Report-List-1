<p><p>The cardiac implantable device market encompasses various technologies aimed at managing heart conditions. Pacemakers are devices that regulate heart rhythms by delivering electrical impulses. Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators (ICDs) monitor heart activity and deliver shocks if dangerous arrhythmias occur, effectively preventing sudden cardiac death. Cardiac Loop Recorders are small devices that continuously monitor and record heart rhythms, aiding in diagnosing intermittent arrhythmias. Together, these devices play a crucial role in improving patient outcomes and managing cardiovascular diseases.</p></p>

<p><p>The fluid-applied waterproofing membrane market is segmented into two main types: cold fluid-applied and hot fluid-applied waterproofing membranes. Cold fluid-applied membranes are applied at ambient temperatures, allowing for easier handling and quicker application, making them suitable for various substrates. In contrast, hot fluid-applied membranes are applied at elevated temperatures, which enhances adhesive properties and durability, ideal for large-scale projects. Both types provide excellent waterproofing solutions, ensuring protection against moisture penetration in construction and infrastructure applications.</p></p>

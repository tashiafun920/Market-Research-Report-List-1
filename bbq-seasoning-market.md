<p><p>The BBQ seasoning market consists of two primary types: dry seasoning and wet seasoning. Dry seasonings are blends of herbs, spices, and other flavoring agents that are typically packaged in a powder or granule form, providing convenience and longer shelf life. Wet seasonings, on the other hand, include marinades and sauces, often incorporating oils, vinegar, or other liquids, adding moisture and flavor during the cooking process. Both types cater to diverse culinary preferences and enhance barbecue experiences.</p></p>

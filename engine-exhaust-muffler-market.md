<p><p>The engine exhaust muffler market comprises several types, primarily including absorption exhaust mufflers, reactive exhaust mufflers, and others. Absorption mufflers utilize sound-absorbing materials to reduce noise by dissipating sound waves. Reactive mufflers work by using chambers and bends to reflect sound waves, canceling out certain frequencies. Other types may include hybrid designs or specialized mufflers for specific applications. Each type serves to minimize engine noise while ensuring efficient exhaust flow, catering to diverse automotive and industrial needs.</p></p>

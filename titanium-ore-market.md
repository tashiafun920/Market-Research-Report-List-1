<p><p>The titanium ore market primarily revolves around three key types: Ilmenite, Titanium Slag, and Rutile. Ilmenite, the most abundant, is a primary source for titanium dioxide production, used in pigments and industrial applications. Titanium Slag, derived from ilmenite through smelting, has higher titanium content and is crucial for producing titanium metal and pigments. Rutile, a naturally high-grade titanium ore, is valued for its purity and is directly used in titanium metal production and welding rod coatings. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, driving demand across sectors.</p></p>

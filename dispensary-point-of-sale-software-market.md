<p><p>Dispensary point-of-sale (POS) software is categorized into two primary types: on-premise and cloud-based systems. On-premise POS software is installed locally on the dispensary's hardware, offering complete control and security but requiring upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based POS software operates over the internet, allowing for remote access and automatic updates, which enhances scalability and reduces hardware costs. Both types aim to streamline operations, manage inventory, and enhance customer service in dispensaries.</p></p>

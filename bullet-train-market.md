<p><strong>The Bullet Train Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wheel on Rail</li><li>Maglev</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The **Bullet Train Market** primarily operates on two technologies: **Wheel on Rail** and **Maglev**. **Wheel on Rail** systems use traditional steel wheels on tracks, offering high-speed travel with proven reliability and lower infrastructure costs. **Maglev** (Magnetic Levitation) trains use magnetic fields to levitate above the tracks, eliminating friction, enabling ultra-high speeds, and reducing noise. While Wheel on Rail dominates due to widespread adoption, Maglev is gaining traction for its futuristic appeal and efficiency, though it requires higher initial investment and specialized infrastructure.</p></p>

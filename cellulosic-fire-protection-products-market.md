<p><strong>The Cellulosic Fire Protection Products Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-borne</li><li>Solvent-borne</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cellulosic fire protection products can be categorized into two main market types: water-borne and solvent-borne. Water-borne products use water as the primary solvent, offering lower VOC emissions, easier cleanup, and safer application. These are ideal for environments needing compliance with stricter environmental regulations. In contrast, solvent-borne products rely on organic solvents, providing better penetration and adhesion to surfaces, making them suitable for challenging applications. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing fire safety in various construction and industrial settings.</p></p>

<p><p>The degradable garbage bags market comprises two main types: biodegradable materials and photodegradable materials. Biodegradable bags are made from organic substances that decompose through natural processes, providing an environmentally friendly alternative to traditional plastic. In contrast, photodegradable bags are designed to break down when exposed to sunlight, utilizing UV radiation to accelerate the degradation process. Both types aim to reduce plastic waste and its environmental impact, although their decomposition mechanisms and applications differ significantly.</p></p>

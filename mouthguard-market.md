<p><p>The mouthguard market features three main types: preformed, thermoformed, and custom mouthguards. Preformed mouthguards are ready-made and offer basic protection, suitable for casual use. Thermoformed mouthguards are soft plastic that, when heated, conform to the user's teeth for a better fit. Custom mouthguards, made by dental professionals, offer the highest level of comfort and protection, tailored specifically to an individual's dental structure. Each type caters to different needs in sports and dental safety.</p></p>

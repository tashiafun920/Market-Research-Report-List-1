<p><p>The wireless barcode scanner market can be categorized into WiFi-based and cellular-based types. WiFi-based scanners connect to local networks, enabling real-time data transfer within range, making them ideal for inventory management in warehouses and retail environments. Cellular-based scanners utilize mobile data networks, offering broader coverage and flexibility for outdoor or remote applications, such as field service or delivery operations. Both types enhance operational efficiency and streamline processes by eliminating the need for wired connections.</p></p>

<p><p>The handheld spectrometer market is primarily divided into two main types: Optical Spectrometers and Electromagnetic Spectrometers. Optical spectrometers analyze light in various wavelengths to determine material composition, enabling applications such as environmental monitoring and quality control. Electromagnetic spectrometers, on the other hand, evaluate a wider range of electromagnetic radiation beyond visible light, often used in fields like astronomy and remote sensing. Both types provide portability and versatility, making them essential tools for real-time analysis across multiple industries.</p></p>

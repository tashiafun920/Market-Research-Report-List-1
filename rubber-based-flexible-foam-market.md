<p><p>The rubber-based flexible foam market includes various types such as Nitrile Butadiene Rubber (NBR), Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer (EPDM), and Chloroprene. NBR foam, known for its oil resistance, is widely used in automotive and industrial applications. EPDM foam offers excellent weather resistance, making it ideal for outdoor products. Chloroprene foam provides flame resistance and insulation properties, commonly used in HVAC and electrical applications. Other types may include blends or specialty foams tailored for specific uses across various industries.</p></p>

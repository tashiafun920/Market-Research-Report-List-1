<p><strong>The Sequencing Platform Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Table</li><li>Large Scale</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sequencing platforms in the market can be categorized mainly into table-top and large-scale market types. Table-top sequencing platforms are compact and designed for smaller laboratories or clinical settings, offering user-friendly operations and lower throughput, making them suitable for targeted applications. Conversely, large-scale sequencing platforms are high-capacity systems capable of processing vast amounts of samples simultaneously, often employed in research institutions and genomics facilities for extensive projects like whole-genome sequencing or population genomics, where throughput and efficiency are paramount.</p></p>

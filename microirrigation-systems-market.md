<p><ul><li>Drip Irrigation</li><li>Micro Spray Irrigation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Microirrigation systems encompass various efficient watering methods, primarily including drip irrigation and micro spray irrigation. Drip irrigation delivers precise amounts of water directly to the plant roots through a network of tubes and emitters, minimizing evaporation and runoff. In contrast, micro spray irrigation employs a set of small spray heads to distribute water in a fine mist over a broader area, ensuring even coverage and reducing water waste. Both systems are essential for optimizing water usage in agriculture and landscaping.</p></p>

<p><p>The chemical special filters market includes activated carbon filters and ion-exchange resin filters, both essential for water and air purification. Activated carbon filters are used to adsorb pollutants, eliminating volatile organic compounds, odors, and chlorine. Ion-exchange resin filters remove specific ions, such as heavy metals or hardness ions, by replacing them with less harmful ones. These filtration methods are crucial in various applications, including industrial processes, wastewater treatment, and residential water purification, ensuring high-quality water and air.</p></p>

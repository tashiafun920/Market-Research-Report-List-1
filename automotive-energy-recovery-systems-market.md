<p><p>The Automotive Energy Recovery Systems market includes technologies designed to capture and reuse energy that would otherwise be lost. The **Automotive Regenerative Braking System** converts kinetic energy during braking into electrical energy, storing it for later use. The **Automotive Waste Heat Recovery System** captures thermal energy from exhaust gases or engine heat, converting it into usable power. The **Kinetic Energy Recovery System (KERS)** stores energy from vehicle motion, often in flywheels or batteries, to boost performance or efficiency. These systems enhance fuel efficiency and reduce emissions.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Seamless Extruded Aluminum Tubing</li><li>Ordinary Extruded Aluminum Tubing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The extruded aluminum tubing market comprises two primary types: seamless extruded aluminum tubing and ordinary extruded aluminum tubing. Seamless tubing is produced without welded joints, offering enhanced strength and corrosion resistance, making it suitable for high-pressure applications. In contrast, ordinary extruded aluminum tubing involves the extrusion process where aluminum alloy is pushed through a die, resulting in joined sections. This type is often used in applications where flexibility and lower costs are prioritized, such as in construction and HVAC systems.</p></p>

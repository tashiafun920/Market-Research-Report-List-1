<p><p>An image intensifier is an advanced optoelectronic device that amplifies low-light images, making them visible to the human eye. By converting photons into electrons and amplifying them, image intensifiers enhance visibility in dark or low-light conditions.</p><p>The Image Intensifier Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13% during the forecast period.
